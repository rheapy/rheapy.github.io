Understanding the Rise of Population in India

This is my final project for PP434: Automated Data Visualization for Public Policy.

Aim: The aim of this project is to explore the factors driving global population growth and its impact on socioeconomic systems. It will analyze key determinants such as birth rates, healthcare, migration, and policies that influence population dynamics. The project also aims to assess the effects of rising populations on resources like food, water, housing, and energy, as well as pressures on healthcare, education, and employment.
